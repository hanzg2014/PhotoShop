# WelcomeUchiwa
The design of a Japanese fan(団扇/うちわ/"uchiwa") to welcome to our lab the exchange student 
Christiane Schwarzl from Austria.

This used both as the welcome poster at Haneda Airport, and as my personal souvenir.
